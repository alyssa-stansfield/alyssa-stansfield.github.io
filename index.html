Alyssa Stansfield, PhD

I'm currently a postdoc at Stony Brook University working with Dr. Kevin Reed in the Climate Extremes Modeling Group. In the Fall, I'll be starting as an NSF AGS Postdoctoral Fellow at Colorado State University with Dr. Kristen Rasmussen. My research interests include tropical cyclones and other extreme weather, climate change and variability, and climate model assessment.



Recent News:

  • May 2022: Gave an oral presentation at the AMS 35th Conference on Hurricanes and Tropical Meteorology and won an outstanding oral presentation award.
  • May 2022: Graduated from Stony Brook University with a PhD in Atmospheric Science and won the Alumni Association’s Dean’s Choice Award for Leadership and the Nuria Protopopescu Memorial Teaching Award.
  • March 2022: Successfully defended my PhD dissertation entitled “An Exploration of Tropical Cyclone Precipitation Under Climate Change Forcings”
